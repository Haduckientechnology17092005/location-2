HOI AN TRAVEL TOUR GUIDE

2.Market tour & Cooking Class (price : 550.000VND)

A fun and interesting way to experience the rural life in Hoi An and Vietnamese cuisine.

Details:

Pickup

Picked up by local tour guides at the Meeting Point (Godiva - Dinks & Cakes at 11 Hoang Dieu Street, Hoi An )
Walk around 30’ at Hoi An Market, you can feel the bustle of the market, have an opportunity to have a direct contact with sellers. Our tour guides will introduce to you the necessary ingredients for cooking classes.

Action

Participate in cooking classes with our local chefs. You will learn how to cook Vietnamese food and share valuable information in Vietnamese cultural cuisine. Then you can enjoy your Vietnamese food.
Finish & back to the Meeting Point.

This is our menu (take it for reference)

Banh cuon (Steamed rice paper rolls)
Banh xeo (Crispy rice pancake)
Pho (Noodles soup)
Che (Mung bean sweet soup)
Note: Please advise any specific dietary requirements at time of booking and the menu sometimes could be changed depending on reality conditions
